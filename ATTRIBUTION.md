## Historical & Research Sources

- **Manitoba Métis Federation (MMF)** — *Manitoba Métis Knowledge, Land Use and Occupancy Study* (2022)  
  Source of waterways, cart trails, and settlement location data.

- **Barkwell, Lawrence J.** — *20th Century Métis Displacement and Road Allowance Communities in Manitoba* (Louis Riel Institute, Nov 8, 2016)  
  Source for road allowance community histories.

- **Barkwell, Lawrence.** — *Historic Métis Settlements in Manitoba and Geographical Place Names* (Louis Riel Institute, 2018)  
  Source for settlement founding dates and community types.

- **Hornaday, William Temple.** — *The Extermination of the American Bison* (Government Printing Office, 1889)  
  Source for buffalo herd polygon data and historical context.
